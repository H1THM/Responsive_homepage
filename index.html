About me

A 29-year-old Portuguese defensive midfielder for Al-Hilal and the Portugal national team. Known for his elite passing and signature long-range goals, the former FC Porto and Wolverhampton Wanderers captain moved to Riyadh in 2023, quickly becoming a crucial midfield anchor in Al-Hilal’s dominant Saudi Pro League and King's Cup title runs.
